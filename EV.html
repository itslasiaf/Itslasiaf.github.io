EV Vehicle

Electric vehicles (EVs) have a battery instead of a gasoline tank, and an electric motor instead of an internal combustion engine, they provide the same purpose as a regular car and in most cases outpreform them.

Anatomy

The anatomy of an EV consists of 3 main parts different from a gas vehicle:

  • EVB
  • Converter
  • Motor

EVB

An electric vehicle battery is a rechargeable battery used to power the electric motors of an electric vehicle.

Converter

A converter converts higher-voltage DC power from the battery pack to the lower-voltage DC power needed to run the vehicle.

Motor

Using power from the battery pack the motor drives the vehicle's wheels.
